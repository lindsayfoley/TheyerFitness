My background......

Hi, I'm Jo Theyer. I believe that anyone can get healthy and fit with a regular, well structured programme of exercise and good nutrition. I studied a sports degree at university, and this has given me an in depth knowledge of how the body works, its functions and more importantly, how to apply this science and theory to practice.

It has given me a great underlying knowledge to take forward into my chosen career path as a personal trainer and a GP & exercise referralist.

To date my best personal achievement has been completing Brighton marathon in just over 4 hours. I have also represented my county and welsh universities at senior level in Badminton.

How can i help...

Whether you are looking for general fitness, weight loss, to rehabilitate and recover from injury or prepare yourself for a sporting event, I can help you achieve your goals.

Personal training to me is a lifestyle rather than a job.
I strive to provide clients with the required materials to achieve their goals, whether these goals are to loose weight, tone up or bulk up. Through a carefully tailored training and nutrition plan you could be only a step away from a new you.

From absolute beginners to experienced athletes, every client will receive a service tailored to their unique needs, targets and budget.
